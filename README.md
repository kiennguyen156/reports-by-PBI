## 📁 About the project

### 📦 Olist E-commerce Business Performance Report
A comprehensive, end-to-end operational report and dashboard focusing on market-driven KPIs, logistics health, and merchant performance across Brazil.
* **High-Level Operations Monitoring:** Developed an enterprise-grade dashboard analyzing **94,800+ transactions and 96,000+ customers** across 140 countries to track GMV, transaction volumes, and category growth.
* **Supply Chain & Logistics Evaluation:** Evaluated service quality across **660+ active merchants**, isolating a critical logistics bottleneck where **55.49% of orders suffered from delayed deliveries** despite maintaining an average product review score of 4.0 - 4.3.
* **Commercial Performance Tracking:** Monitored monthly sales velocity to reveal that the *Health & Beauty* and *Watches & Gifts* categories dominated the platform's revenue while yielding exceptional profit margins exceeding 91%.
* **Behavioral Health Segmentation:** Integrated an RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) behavioral model to monitor user base stagnation, identifying that the **"At Risk" and "Hibernating" segments combined accounted for 53.3%** of total monetary value.
